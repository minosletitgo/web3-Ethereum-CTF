### Phoenixtto
- Within the world of crossovers there is a special one, where the universes of pokemon, harry potter and solidity intertwine.
- In this crossover a mixed creature is created between dumbledore's phoenix, a wild ditto and since we are in the solidity universe this creature is a contract.
- We have called it Phoenixtto and it has two important abilities, that of being reborn from it's ashes after its destruction and that of copying the behavior of another bytecode.
- Try to capture the Phoenixtto, if you can...
