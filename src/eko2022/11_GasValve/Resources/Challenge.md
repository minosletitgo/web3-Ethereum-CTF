### Gas Valve
- The evil Dr. N. Gas has put into orbit a machine that can suck all the air out of the atmosphere.
- You sneaked into his spaceship and must find a nozzle to open the main valve and stop the machine!
- Assert the situation and don't panic. Hint: on the valve is marked "model no. EIP-150"

### 燃气阀门
- 邪恶的 N. Gas 博士将一台能吸走大气中所有空气的机器送入了轨道。
- 你潜入了他的飞船，必须找到一个喷嘴来打开主阀门，阻止这台机器！
- 评估情况，不要惊慌。提示：阀门上标记着“型号：EIP-150”
