### The Lost Kitty
- Lucas is a scientist who lives with his cat in a big house that has 2^256 rooms.
- His cat likes to play hide and seek and jumps to a random room whenever it hears a door opening in another one.
- Can you find Lucas' cat? Set the variable **catFound** to **true** to win this challenge.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 迷失的小猫
- 卢卡斯是一位科学家，他和他的猫住在一栋有 2²⁵⁶ 个房间的大房子里。
- 他的猫喜欢玩捉迷藏，每当听到其他房间的门打开时，就会跳到一个随机的房间里。
- 你能找到卢卡斯的猫吗？将变量 **catFound** 设置为 **true** 即可赢得本挑战。
